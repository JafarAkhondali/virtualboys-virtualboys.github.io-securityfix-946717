Meet SweeTango, an original NFT project in the Fantom ecosystem. Mint your own SweeTango into existence where it will wander around and play with other people's Swees until the end of time. Thru blockchain tech, your Swee will outlive you and everyone you love.

Play Online. Twitter.


SweeTango is an NFT game where you manage a crew of Swee's. Send them on adventures, play minigames, & decorate their playpen. They have no idea what's going on. It's a miracle the power to the minter is still hooked up.
